New Zealand Nationals 2025

Registration requirements for the competition:

Register for this competition below.
There is a competitor limit of 300 competitors.
The base registration fee for this competition is $70 (New Zealand Dollar).
The registration fee has to be paid through Stripe below once registered.
If your registration is cancelled before Sunday, November 30, 2025 at 11:59 AM GMT+1 you will be refunded 100% of your registration fee.
Registrants on the waiting list may be accepted onto the competitor list until Monday, December 8, 2025 at 7:00 AM GMT+1.
If you are a registered competitor you may change your registered events until Sunday, November 30, 2025 at 11:59 AM GMT+1 on the Register tab.
No on the spot registrations will be accepted.
Any spectator can attend for free.
Competitors may only register for events that they are already qualified for.
